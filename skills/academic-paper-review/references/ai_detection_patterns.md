# AI Writing Detection Patterns for Academic Papers

This document compiles AI writing detection patterns derived from the humanizer skill and adapted for academic paper review. These patterns help identify sections that may have been generated by AI tools or exhibit AI-like writing characteristics.

---

## Content Patterns

### 1. Undue Emphasis on Significance (过度强调重要性)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 标志着, 证明了, 突出显示, 强调了, 体现了, 反映了, 象征着, 贡献, 铺垫, 标志, 关键转折点, 深远影响, 不可磨灭的印记, 深深扎根
- English: stands/serves as, is a testament/reminder, vital/significant/crucial/pivotal/key role/moment, underscores/highlights its importance/significance, reflects broader, symbolizing its ongoing/enduring/lasting, contributing to, setting the stage for, marking/shaping, represents/marks a shift, key turning point, evolving landscape, focal point, indelible mark, deeply rooted

**Problem:** AI text inflates importance by adding statements about how arbitrary aspects represent or contribute to broader topics.

**Example in academic context:**
> "本研究标志着学生参与度演变的关键时刻，为高等教育改革做出了重要贡献。"
>
> "This study marks a pivotal moment in the evolution of student engagement, contributing significantly to higher education reform."

**Fix:** State what was actually found or done:
> "本研究发现，参与课外活动的学生平均GPA高出0.3分。"
>
> "This study found that students participating in extracurricular activities had an average GPA 0.3 points higher."

---

### 2. Undue Emphasis on Notability (过度强调重要性/知名度)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 独立报道, 地方/区域/国家级媒体 outlets, 由领先专家撰写, 活跃的社交媒体存在
- English: independent coverage, local/regional/national media outlets, written by a leading expert, active social media presence

**Problem:** AI hits readers over the head with claims of notability, often listing sources without context.

**Example:**
> "本研究已被《Nature》、《Science》、《Cell》等顶级期刊引用。作者在社交媒体上拥有超过50万关注者。"

**Fix:** Provide specific, meaningful citation:
> "在2024年《Nature》的一篇评论文章中，Smith等人引用本研究作为支持该理论的主要证据之一。"

---

### 3. Superficial Analyses with -ing Endings (肤浅的-ing短语分析)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 强调/突出/显示..., 确保..., 反映/象征..., 贡献给..., 培养/促进..., 包含..., 展示...
- English: highlighting/underscoring/emphasizing..., ensuring..., reflecting/symbolizing..., contributing to..., cultivating/fostering..., encompassing..., showcasing...

**Problem:** AI tacks present participle ("-ing") phrases onto sentences to add fake depth.

**Example:**
> "该模型采用了蓝色的配色方案，象征着该地区的自然美景，反映了作者对环境保护的深切关注，强调了可持续发展在学术研究中的重要性。"

**Fix:** State directly:
> "该模型使用蓝色配色方案。作者表示选择此颜色是为了强调环境可持续性。"

---

### 4. Promotional Language (宣传性语言)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 以...为荣, 充满活力, 丰富（比喻义）, 深刻, 增强, 展示, 体现, 致力于, 自然美, 坐落于, 中心地带, 开创性（比喻义）, 著名, 惊人, 必访, 令人惊叹
- English: boasts a, vibrant, rich (figurative), profound, enhancing its, showcasing, exemplifies, commitment to, natural beauty, nestled, in the heart of, groundbreaking (figurative), renowned, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning

**Problem:** AI struggles to maintain neutral tone.

**Example:**
> "本研究采用开创性的方法，展现了令人惊叹的结果，为该领域做出了深刻贡献。"

**Fix:**
> "本研究采用了混合方法设计。结果显示，实验组的响应率比对照组高23%。"

---

### 5. Vague Attributions (模糊归因)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 行业报告, 观察家引用, 专家认为, 一些批评家认为, 几个来源/出版物（实际引用很少时）
- English: Industry reports, Observers have cited, Experts argue, Some critics argue, several sources/publications

**Problem:** AI attributes opinions to vague authorities without specific sources.

**Example:**
> "由于独特特征，该算法引起研究者和保护主义者的兴趣。专家认为它在区域生态系统中发挥关键作用。"

**Fix:** Provide specific attribution:
> "根据中国科学院2019年的一项调查，该算法使区域生态预测准确度提高了15%。"

---

### 6. Formulaic "Challenges and Future Prospects" Sections (程式化的"挑战与展望")

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 尽管其...面临几个挑战, 尽管存在这些挑战, 挑战与遗产, 未来展望
- English: Despite its... faces several challenges, Despite these challenges, Challenges and Legacy, Future Outlook

**Problem:** Many AI-generated articles include formulaic "Challenges" sections.

**Example:**
> "尽管本研究取得了令人鼓舞的结果，但仍面临几个挑战，包括数据局限性和需要进一步验证。尽管存在这些挑战，凭借其创新的approach和持续的initiatives，该研究继续蓬勃发展，成为该领域不可或缺的一部分。"

**Fix:** Be specific about actual limitations and next steps:
> "本研究的样本量仅限于单个机构的参与者。未来的研究应包括多个机构以验证结果的普遍性。"

## Language and Grammar Patterns

### 7. Overused AI Vocabulary Words (过度使用的AI词汇)

**High-frequency AI words:**
- Chinese: 此外, 与...一致, 关键, 深入, 强调, 持久, 增强, 培养, 获得, 突出（动词）, 相互作用, 错综复杂/错综, 关键（形容词）, 景观（抽象名词）, 关键, 展示, 织锦（抽象名词）, 见证, 证明, 有价值, 充满活力
- English: Additionally, align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight (verb), interplay, intricate/intricacies, key (adjective), landscape (abstract noun), pivotal, showcase, tapestry (abstract noun), testament, underscore (verb), valuable, vibrant

**Problem:** These words appear far more frequently in post-2023 text. They often co-occur.

**Example:**
> "此外，该研究的一个独特特征是采用了创新方法。与传统影响的持久见证是当地文化景观中广泛采用的这种approach，展示这些dish如何融入传统diet，突显了技术与传统之间的错综复杂的相互作用。"

**Fix:** Use simpler, more direct language:
> "本研究还采用了混合方法设计。这种方法在2020年后变得普遍。研究结果表明，当技术培训与传统实践相结合时，参与者采用率会提高。"

---

### 8. Copula Avoidance (回避使用"是"/"are")

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 充当, 作为, 标志, 代表[一个], 以...为荣, 提供[一个]
- English: serves as/stands as/marks/represents [a], boasts/features/offers [a]

**Problem:** AI substitutes elaborate constructions for simple copulas.

**Example:**
> "该实验室作为我们当代艺术展览空间。该实验室提供四个独立空间，以超过3000平方英尺为荣。"

**Fix:** Use "is/has":
> "该实验室是我们的当代艺术展览空间。实验室共有四个房间，总面积3000平方英尺。"

---

### 9. Negative Parallelisms (否定平行结构)

**Problem:** Constructions like "Not only...but also..." or "It's not just..., it's..." are overused.

**Example:**
> "这不仅仅关于工具的选择；这关乎整个研究范式。这不仅是一项研究；这是一份声明。"

**Fix:**
> "工具的选择直接影响研究范式。本研究的核心发现挑战了该领域的主流观点。"

---

### 10. Rule of Three Overuse (过多使用"三的法则")

**Problem:** AI forces ideas into groups of three to appear comprehensive.

**Example:**
> "该研究包括主题session、panel讨论和网络机会。参与者可以期待创新、灵感和行业见解。"

**Fix:**
> "该研究包括talk和panel。session之间还有非正式networking时间。"

---

### 11. Elegant Variation (优雅变奏/同义词循环)

**Problem:** AI has repetition-penalty code causing excessive synonym substitution.

**Example:**
> "主角面临许多挑战。主要character必须克服obstacles。中心figure最终triumphs。英雄回归家。"

**Fix:**
> "主角面临许多挑战，但最终凯旋并返家。"

---

### 12. False Ranges (虚假范围)

**Problem:** AI uses "from X to Y" constructions where X and Y aren't on a meaningful scale.

**Example:**
> "我们的宇宙之旅将我们从大爆炸的奇点带到了宏伟的宇宙网，从恒星的诞生和死亡到暗物质的神秘舞蹈。"

**Fix:**
> "本书涵盖了大爆炸、恒星形成和关于暗物质的当前理论。"

---

## Style Patterns

### 13. Em Dash Overuse (过度使用破折号)

**Problem:** AI uses em dashes (—) more than humans, mimicking "punchy" sales writing.

**Example:**
> "该术语主要由荷兰机构推广——而不是由人民自己。你不会说"荷兰，欧洲"作为地址——但这种错误标记仍在继续——甚至在官方文件中也是如此。"

**Fix:**
> "该术语主要由荷兰机构推广，而不是由人民自己。你不会说"荷兰，欧洲"作为地址，但这种错误标记在官方文件中仍在继续。"

---

### 14. Overuse of Boldface (过度使用粗体)

**Problem:** AI emphasizes phrases in boldface mechanically.

**Example:**
> "它融合了**OKRs（目标与关键结果）**、**KPIs（关键绩效指标）**以及诸如**商业模式画布（BMC）**和**平衡记分卡（BSC）**等视觉战略工具。"

**Fix:**
> "它融合了OKRs、KPIs以及商业模式画布和平衡记分卡等视觉战略工具。"

---

### 15. Inline-Header Vertical Lists (内联标题垂直列表)

**Problem:** AI outputs lists where items start with bolded headers followed by colons.

**Example:**
> - **用户体验：** 用户体验通过新界面得到显著改善。
> - **性能：** 性能通过优化算法得到增强。
> - **安全性：** 安全性通过端到端加密得到加强。

**Fix:**
> "该更新改善了界面，通过优化算法加快加载时间，并添加了端到端加密。"

---

### 16. Title Case in Headings (标题中的首字母大写)

**Problem:** AI chatbots capitalize all main words in headings (English).

**Example:**
> ## Strategic Negotiations And Global Partnerships

**Fix:**
> ## Strategic negotiations and global partnerships

---

### 17. Emojis (表情符号)

**Problem:** AI chatbots often decorate headings or bullet points with emojis.

**Example:**
> - **发布阶段：** 产品将在第三季度发布
> - **关键见解：** 用户更喜欢简单性
> - **下一步：** 安排后续会议

**Fix:** Remove emojis:
> "产品将在第三季度发布。用户研究显示对简单性的偏好。下一步：安排后续会议。"

---

## Communication Patterns

### 18. Collaborative Communication Artifacts (协作通信痕迹)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 希望这有所帮助, 当然!, 当然可以!, 您完全正确!, 您想要..., 让我知道, 这里是...
- English: I hope this helps, Of course!, Certainly!, You're absolutely right!, Would you like..., let me know, here is a...

**Problem:** Chatbot correspondence gets pasted as content.

**Example:**
> "以下是法国革命的概述。希望这有所帮助！如果您希望我扩展任何section，请告诉我。"

**Fix:**
> "法国革命始于1789年，当时金融危机和食物短缺导致广泛动荡。"

---

### 19. Knowledge-Cutoff Disclaimers (知识截止声明)

**Words to watch:**
- Chinese: 截至[日期], 截至我上次训练更新, 虽然具体细节有限/稀缺..., 基于可用信息...
- English: as of [date], Up to my last training update, While specific details are limited/scarce..., based on available information...

**Problem:** AI disclaimers about incomplete information get left in text.

**Example:**
> "虽然关于该公司成立的细节在广泛可用的来源中没有充分记录，但它似乎是在1990年代的某个时间建立的。"

**Fix:**
> "该公司成立于1994年，根据其注册文件。"

---

### 20. Sycophantic/Servile Tone (谄媚/奴性语气)

**Problem:** Overly positive, people-pleasing language.

**Example:**
> "好问题！您完全正确，这是一个复杂的话题。关于经济因素，那是一个很好的观点。"

**Fix:**
> "您提到的经济因素在这里是相关的。"

## Academic-Specific AI Patterns

### 21. Hedging in Academic Claims (学术主张中的模糊表达)

**Problem:** AI overuses cautious language in academic contexts.

**Examples:**
- "似乎表明" instead of stating findings directly
- "可能解释了" when results are clear
- "在一定程度上" unnecessarily added
- "某种程度上" used excessively

**Fix:** Be direct about findings:
- "结果表明" not "似乎表明"
- "这解释了" when evidence is strong
- Remove unnecessary qualifiers

### 23. Generic Academic Phrases (通用学术短语)

**Problem:** AI relies on generic academic filler.

**Examples to avoid:**
- "在当今的学术景观中"
- "越来越多的证据表明" (without specific citations)
- "值得进一步研究" (when no actual follow-up suggested)
- "本研究具有重要的理论和实践意义" (without specifying what)
- "为...提供了新的视角" (without explaining the perspective)

**Fix:** Be specific:
- "自2020年以来，五项研究表明..."
- "未来的研究应该考察X对Y的影响"
- "本研究的主要贡献是..."
- "该框架使研究人员能够区分A和B"

---

### 24. Overly Complex Sentence Structures (过于复杂的句子结构)

**Problem:** AI creates unnecessarily complex academic sentences.

**Example:**
> "通过采用方法论框架，使我们能够考察变量之间的关系，从而为理解现象提供了见解，本研究揭示了..."

**Fix:** Break into simpler sentences:
> "本研究采用X方法论框架。该方法使我们能够考察A和B之间的关系。研究结果表明..."

---

### 25. Repetitive Citation Patterns (重复的引用模式)

**Problem:** AI uses formulaic citation patterns.

**Examples:**
- "正如Smith (2020)指出的那样，..." (overused)
- "先前的研究已经表明..." (without specific citations)
- Citing the same sources repeatedly
- Citations added without meaningful integration

**Fix:** Integrate citations meaningfully:
- "Smith (2020)发现X与Y相关，这与Jones (2019)的观察一致，即..."
- "五项近期研究（Smith等，2020；Jones，2021；...）报告了类似的结果"

## Detection Checklist

When reviewing academic papers for AI writing patterns, check for:

- [ ] Multiple instances of AI vocabulary words in close proximity
- [ ] Overuse of em dashes in academic text
- [ ] Formulaic transitions (首先，其次，最后)
- [ ] Vague attributions (专家认为, 先前研究)
- [ ] Promotional language (开创性, 令人惊叹)
- [ ] Significance inflation (关键作用, 重要贡献 without specifics)
- [ ] Copula avoidance (充当, 作为, 标志)
- [ ] Negative parallelisms (不仅仅是..., 而是...）
- [ ] Rule-of-three patterns
- [ ] Generic conclusions (需要进一步研究 without specifics)
- [ ] Knowledge-cutoff disclaimers
- [ ] Collaborative artifacts (希望这有所帮助）
- [ ] Excessive hedging (似乎, 可能, 某种程度)

If 3+ patterns are detected in a section, flag for potential AI generation and provide humanizing revisions.
